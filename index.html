Projects

Credit Card Fraud Detection with Variational Autoencoder

January 2025

Developed a VAE-based anomaly detection model using Pyro and a KL Annealing structure applied to a heavily imbalanced financial transaction dataset. The model achieved a recall of 97.3% while maintaining high specificity. Benchmark comparisons with models like LightGBM and Random Forest validated its performance.
